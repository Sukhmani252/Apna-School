Rules And Regulations

General Rules

  • No bullying or harassment of any kind is tolerated.
  • All students must be in their classrooms before the bell rings.
  • Cell phones and other electronic devices are not allowed during class hours.
  • Respectful behavior is expected towards teachers, staff, and fellow students.

Dress Code

All students are required to adhere to the school's dress code policy:

  • Wear clean and appropriate clothing.
  • Avoid clothing with offensive language or images.
  • Shorts and skirts must be of appropriate length.
  • No hats or head coverings are allowed indoors.
