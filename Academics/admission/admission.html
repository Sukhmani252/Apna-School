Admissions - Procedure

Admissions at Laurels School International are based on the following procedure:

  • Priority of registration
  • Results in the Admission
  • Results in the Admission Test / Interview

The admission test is conducted at the level of the Annual Examination of the class preceding the one for which admission is being sought.

Admission/Registration fee is non-adjustable and non-refundable.

Registration of a student does not guarantee admission in Laurels School International.

The management reserves the right to grant/reject admission without assigning any reason.

Documentation

Admission Form provided along with the Prospectus, must be filled in and submitted along with the admission / registration fee in advance with the following documents:

  • A copy of the birth certificate attested by a Gazetted Officer.
  • A copy of the Progress Report of the previous school.
  • The Original Transfer Certificate countersigned by the respective Education Board (once admission is confirmed).
  • A copy of the Aadhar Card.

If a student belongs to SC, ST, or OBC, the caste certificate should be submitted at the time of admission.

75% attendance is compulsory for the students seeking admission in Classes IX to XII.
